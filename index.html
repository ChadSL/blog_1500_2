Billions being spent in metaverse land grab


BBC News | November 3, 2022

Billions of dollars worth of cryptocurrency has been spent buying virtual land in Mark Zuckerberg's Metaverse.
$1.93 billion has been spent on land in this past year alone. Land is being purchased in these online worlds mainly for self promotion, stores, galleries, and more.
Leaked memos show people do not stay for long although the company is convinced more people will stay as the virtual world develops.
